NuN Central

NuN Central is the command dashboard for governance, resource management, and tokenomics.

  • Unified web dashboard for node operators and users
  • Governance voting and proposals
  • Token allocation, staking, and distribution
  • Resource monitoring (CPU, storage, GPU, RAM)
  • Real-time analytics and notifications

Status: Planned – Launch Q3 2025
